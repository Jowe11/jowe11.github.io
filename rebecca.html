Rebecca Clark

Rebecca is a student of music and the arts. This phrase alone doesn't represent her accomplishments and varied interests. Currently, she is studying: Ballet, violin, harp, piano, and voice. She also plays flute and organ on occasion at church. Recently she performed an hour recital in each of the musical fields. She has become quite accomplished within each of these areas of study.
Rebecca is very active in The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. She has served as Young Women's class Secretary, and currently serves as Young Women's class President and Young Women's Music Specialist.
Among these accomplishments, her passions are: art, reading, poetry, and spending time with family.
